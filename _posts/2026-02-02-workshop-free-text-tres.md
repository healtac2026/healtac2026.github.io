We are pleased to announce a **full-day workshop on Free-text healthcare data and Trusted Research Environments (TREs)** at HealTAC 2026.

Funded by **DARE UK**, the workshop will bring together project teams from **STAR-TRE**, **FORTRESS-TeHR**, and **TRExt**, alongside the **SAFETEXT** community working group, to explore next-generation capabilities for the safe and responsible use of de-identified and synthetic healthcare free-text data. The workshop will highlight emerging tools, evaluation practices, and governance considerations, and will provide an opportunity for community feedback and discussion of future collaboration.
